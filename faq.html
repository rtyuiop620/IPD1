Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account on your website?

+

To create an account, click on the “Sign Up” button on the homepage, fill in the necessary details such as your name, email, and password, and then follow the instructions to complete the registration process.

Are the courses free?

+

Yes, the courses are completely free, and you will also receive a free certificate upon completion.

Can I access the course materials on mobile devices?

+

Yes! Our website is fully responsive, so you can access all course materials on your mobile phone, tablet, or computer.

How long do I have access to the course content?

+

You will have lifetime access to the course content once you enroll. This means you can revisit the materials anytime after completing the course.

What should I do if I encounter technical issues during a course?

+

If you face any technical issues, you can contact our support team by emailing support@elearning.com or using the live chat feature on the website.

Are there any prerequisites for taking a course?

+

Some advanced courses may require prior knowledge or experience. Course prerequisites (if any) will be mentioned in the course. Beginner-level courses do not require any prior knowledge.

How are the courses structured?

+

Each course is divided into modules with video lectures, quizzes , challenges , leaderboard and completion certificate. You can proceed at your own pace and track your progress and start from where you left.

What courses does your website offer?

+

We offer a wide range of courses including Basic computer skills , email and communication, Cyber security , E-Commerce and many more. Visit our courses page to explore all available options.

Can I start if I am a beginner?

+

Yes! Our platform offers courses for all levels, including beginner-friendly courses. Each course has clear instructions, and you can progress at your own pace.

Are there advanced-level courses?

+

Yes, we offer advanced-level courses for professionals looking to deepen their knowledge in various fields. Explore our course catalog to find courses that suit your expertise level.

How do I access the course content?

+

Once you sign-up, you can access the content via your homepage. The content is available as video lectures and quizzes, all of which can be accessed at any time.

How can I access the quizzes?

+

Quizzes are available after completing certain lessons or modules. You can access them through the homepage, and they will test your understanding of the content.

What are the steps to complete a course?

+

The steps to complete a course are as follows:

  • Access the course content.
  • Watch and Understand the vedio lectures.
  • Take the quizzes to test your knowledge.
  • Once you finish, you'll be awarded a certificate.

How do I receive a certificate?

+

Upon successful completion of a course, including all assignments and quizzes, you will receive a digital certificate, which can be downloaded and shared on your resume or LinkedIn profile.

Is there a leaderboard to track my progress?

+

Yes, we have a leaderboard where you can compare your progress with other learners. Your rank is determined by your quiz scores, course completions, and overall participation.

How can I track my progress?

+

You can track your progress via the course dashboard. It shows your completed and pending lessons , what amount of progress you made in every lesson and time left to complete that course.
